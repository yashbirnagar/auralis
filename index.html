About us

At Auralis, we are committed to delivering cutting-edge oxygen generation solutions that prioritize health, safety, and sustainability. Whether it's for hospitals, clinics, wellness centers, industrial use, or home care, our oxygen generators are built to perform with precision .

Our Story

Founded on the principles of innovation and sustainability, The journey of Auralis began with a simple but powerful mission — to make life-saving oxygen accessible, affordable, and reliable for everyone.During times when the world faced an oxygen crisis, our team of engineers, healthcare experts, and innovators came together to create a solution that could meet urgent needs without dependency on external supplies.
